What changed
Three moves to elevate the feed
Same brand. Same purple. Same "Me." — but with a point of view that earns the follow.
1 Editorial type
Fraunces + Inter, in conversation.
A serif with a soft italic axis for headlines and quote moments, paired with a neutral sans for UI. Makes every post feel designed, not defaulted.
2 Mixed grid
Photo, quote, tip, product.
Four content pillars weave through the grid — breaking up photo-heavy rows with typography cards, member quotes, and therapist tips so followers always find something new.
3 Unified palette
Periwinkle, soft peach, sage.
The brand purple stays dominant, with warm accent tones (peach, sage, ink) reserved for content pillars. Every photo gets a subtle purple grade so the grid reads as one.
